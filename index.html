Career

Nova Lane is a Brooklyn-based indie-pop singer-songwriter blending dreamy synth textures with confessional lyrics. After building a loyal audience through live rooftop sessions and short-form acoustic clips, Nova released her breakout EP, Streetlight Diaries, in late 2025.

Her single "Midnight Signals" crossed 1 million streams in under four months and earned placements on major editorial playlists. Nova has opened for touring acts across the Northeast and is currently writing her debut full-length album for release in 2027.

About

Raised between Miami and New York City, Nova Lane grew up on a mix of soul classics, electronic pop, and Latin rhythms. Her music explores late-night city life, memory, and self-discovery, pairing vulnerable storytelling with atmospheric production.

Known for intimate performances and cinematic visuals, Nova creates shows that feel both personal and immersive. Offstage, she collaborates with local youth arts programs and mentors emerging songwriters.
